Our Shared Canvas: Screenprinting Project

In the Our Shared Canvas screenprinting project, I learned how designs can be transferred onto different materials using a screenprinting process. During this project, I worked on creating and preparing a design, following instructions carefully, and participating in the printing process.

The 21st-century skill I demonstrated the most was creativity. I had to think about how my design would look when it was printed and make decisions about the overall appearance of the final product.
