HI World!

Hi My name is Yousif Kuwa. I'm 15 years old and I live in New Haven, CT. I'm a student at ESUMS in my 9th year. This is my website about what I worked on this year.

About the Project

Me and my classmates worked on screenprinting. In the screenprinting project I learned how designs can be transferred onto different materials using screenprinting. During this project I worked on creating and preparing a design, following instructions carefully in the printing process. My screen printed design expressed confidence and growth. I wanted to show that sometimes people need a push to become more confident and take risks.

What was used to create the screenprint design

To make the screen print I used Adobe Photoshop. This app allowed me to create my design. My design was about confidence and I drew a plane and someone jumping out of it. The person jumping out of the plane is me. My classmates helped by taking a photo of me using a green blanket and a desk setup. I was positioned to look like I was falling from the sky. I uploaded it to the MacBook and added my message. We also used Adobe Illustrator to create measurements for the frame.
