The message

My message to the world is confidence is strength don’t back down because you might get scared, embarrassed or something wrong. All these things are learning opportunities to grow and to impower your self with the strength and the confidence to continue in life when things get hard. Sometimes you gotta take a stepback to move forward rejection is redirection to something greater.

21st Century Skill

The 21st-century skill I demonstrated the most was creativity. I had to think about how my design would look when it was printed and make decisions about the overall appearance of the final product. This project helped me improve my creativity and problem-solving skills because I had to plan my design carefully and make adjustments throughout the process.
